Biography

I am Samyar Shafiee, an immigrant who moved to Canada in 2022. Despite challenges with my English skills, I won the Grade 8 Math Award and made the Honour Roll the same year. The first year of high school, especially in the IB program, was tough, but I overcame the challenges and improved my average by 10% across all subjects in the second year. My journey is a testament to resilience and hard work.

Research & Projects

In 2024, my friend and I co-authored a research paper titled "The Mysteries of Quantum Physics: Exploring the Foundations, Phenomena, and Technological Implications," which we sent to over 30 universities. Currently, we are working on a crypto-based workout app as a non-profit. The app will reward users and donate subscription earnings to cancer research, creating a meaningful impact in both health and science.
